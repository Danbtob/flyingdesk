What We Do

Research

Ergonomify researches a large variety of ergonomic products, ranging from keyboards and mice to chairs and standing desks, to help find the best options for your office and home workspace.

Review

Ergonomify reviews select ergonomic products that we believe are worth investing your time and money in. We review based on risk-of-injury, comfortability, budget, and looks/feel.

Promote

Ergonomify partners with many ergonomic products sellers and distributors to advertise positively-reviewed products to our users.

Connect

Ergonomify connects our users to sellers and distributors to allow for easy access to buy ergonomic products.
